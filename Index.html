About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, is a Portuguese professional footballer considered one of the greatest players of all time. He has won numerous awards, including five Ballon d'Or trophies and multiple Champions League titles.

Ronaldo's Career

  • 2003-2009: Manchester United (Premier League, Champions League titles)
  • 2009-2018: Real Madrid (4 Champions League, 2 La Liga titles)
  • 2018-2021: Juventus (2 Serie A titles)
  • 2021-Present: Al Nassr (Saudi Pro League)

Ronaldo's Achievements

  • 5x Ballon d'Or Winner (2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017)
  • 5x UEFA Champions League Winner
  • Euro 2016 Winner with Portugal
  • Top Scorer in UEFA Champions League History
  • Hundreds of goals in domestic leagues and international competitions
